FAQ

Do you work with new openings or existing restaurants?

Both. We support new concepts (pre-opening to launch) and existing operations (profit, systems, consistency, turnarounds).

How fast can we see impact?

Typically within weeks once the highest-leverage fixes are implemented (menu engineering, purchasing, prep systems, training).

Is this remote-only?

Remote is available, but on-site audits are recommended when operational execution is the primary issue.
